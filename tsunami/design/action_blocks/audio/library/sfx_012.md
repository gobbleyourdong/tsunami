# sfx_012 — `boss_roar`

**Archetype:** threat-signaling growl / large-creature vocalization
**Shape:** canonical `SfxrParams`.
**Use cases:** boss-encounter-intro, dragon-roar, mini-boss-aggro,
creature-enrage-phase-transition
**Variants:** `boss_roar_deep`, `boss_roar_screech`

## Description

Long low-noise burst (baseFreq: 0.15) with slow-vibrato (speed 0.15,
strength 0.3) — the "warble" of an animal throat. LPF at 0.4 with
resonance 0.35 removes brittle highs and adds body. Flanger opens
slightly over the sustain for "shifting" tension. Slight freqRamp
downward gives a falling-exhalation shape.

Duration ~1.2s — long enough to STOP the player, too short to be
tedious. Ideally triggered ONCE per boss encounter (not looping).
