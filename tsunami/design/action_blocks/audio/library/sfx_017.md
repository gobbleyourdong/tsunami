## Description

Sine wave with large upward pitch ramp (0.45) — starts low (baseFreq
0.15), climbs fast. Fast vibrato (speed 0.8, strength 0.1) gives
shimmering character. Flanger opens over sustain (offset 0.2, ramp
0.15) — "materializing" harmonic thickness. LPF opens (+0.2 ramp)
as the entity fully forms.

Duration ~400ms. The "something appeared" sound.
