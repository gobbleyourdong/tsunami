## Description

Sine wave with gentle vibrato and slow arpeggio. `arpMod: 0.3` +
`arpSpeed: 0.3` gives a 3-note rising figure (like a mini "magic
chime"). No sharp attack — slow fade-in (0.05s). Flanger adds
"shimmering warmth." High baseFreq (0.55) keeps it bright and airy
rather than dark/ominous.

Duration ~700ms. The feel of "being restored."
