# sfx_019 — `dash`

**Archetype:** quick movement / sprint / evade / roll
**Shape:** canonical `SfxrParams`.
**Use cases:** dash-ability, evade-roll, sprint-start, dodge,
teleport-short-distance, afterimage-move
**Variants:** `dash_long`, `dash_reverse` (backstep)

## Description

Short whoosh — noise with strong flanger for "wind rushing past."
FreqRamp -0.25 gives the pitch drop of "moving away from rest."
Flanger opens sharply (offset 0.25, ramp 0.2) creating the
characteristic "swoosh" comb-filter effect. Short overall (~220ms).

This is the kind of SFX that plays constantly in platformers —
needs to be non-fatiguing at high play-rate.

Sharper pitch-drop + shorter duration + reduced flanger = "quick
backstep" feel rather than forward sprint.
