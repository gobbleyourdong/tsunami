**Archetype:** impact (footstep / landing / drop)
**Shape:** canonical `SfxrParams` per synthesis attempt_001 §3.
**Use cases:** character lands from jump, object drops, slam-down,
footstep on firm ground, crate lands

## Description

Low noise burst with big punch, fast decay, and downward pitch ramp
for the "settling" feel. LPF at 0.4 cuts the high end — this is a
*thunk*, not a *crash*. Shorter than `hit_flesh` and with more low-
end weight.

Pairs naturally with `jump` (sfx_004) at platformer-landing moments.

## Variants

**`land_heavy`** (big drop, armor, crate):

Shorter, weaker, softer LPF = muted "pff" for soft surfaces.
