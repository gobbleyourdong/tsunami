## How to hand-tune a flagged payload

1. Open the payload JSON at `scaffolds/.claude/nudges/<essence>/<animation>.json`.
2. Edit the `nudges[]` array: rewrite `delta` to a concrete motion-verb + noun phrase (e.g., 'small yellow spark expands into radial burst' instead of 'next phase of impact').
3. Optionally tighten `base_prompt` (the identity section) if flagged `base_prompt_thin`.
4. Leave `confidence` alone — Qwen doesn't read it; it's just a parser-side audit signal.
5. Re-run `tools/nudge_quality_report.py` to confirm the issue clears.
